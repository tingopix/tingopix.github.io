FAQ - Tscann8 / ALT-Scann8 Differences

ALT-Scann8 is an enhanced software interface and hardware improvements for the Tscann8 hardware. The two share the same mechanical platform; the comparison below notes where ALT-Scann8 differs from the base Tscann8 software.

FeatureTscann8 / ALT-Scann8Tingopix v1
Film formatsSuper 8, Regular 8Super 8, Regular 8, 8mm, 16mm
Reel capacityStandard reelsUp to 400ft (8mm)
CameraRPi HQ (IMX477)RPi HQ (IMX477)
LensIndustrial lens (proprietary mount)Enlarger lens, M42 tube + adapters
IlluminationSingle white LED with diffuserMultiple LEDs with 3D-printed integrating sphere, bespoke PICO control
Frame detectionUV LED emitter + photo detector (hardware)Vision-based — electronic, from sprocket and film edge
Transport typeCapstan with pinch rollerFriction capstan (no pinch roller)
Film handlingPinch roller pressure on filmTension-only drive (no pinch roller)
Film contactFlat rollers, full-width contactProfiled rollers, edge-only contact with film
Tension controlBasicActive, supply to pickup, PICO-controlled
StabilizationPost-scan (ALT-Scann8)Directly at capture — from sprocket and film edge
Motion controllerArduino (Tscann8 / ALT-Scann8)Raspberry Pi Pico (RP2040), F-Code command system
RPi ↔ ControllerI2CUSB serial — simpler wiring
PCBThrough-holeSMD

Image pipelinepicamera2 standard RGBSensel-native — bypasses picamera2 RGB pipeline
